Download the macOS app for Region 2 leadership use on a MacBook or desktop Mac. The app is signed with the Discount Tire Developer ID and notarized by Apple, so it opens cleanly on any current macOS (14 Sonoma or newer) without right-click workarounds.

⬇️ Download Regional Leadership Hub (Mac)

First-time install

  1. Click the download button above. Safari saves the zip to your Downloads folder and unzips it automatically. (In Chrome or Firefox, you may need to double-click the zip after it downloads.)
  2. Drag Regional Leadership Hub.app into your Applications folder.
  3. Double-click the app from Applications to launch it.

Updates

When a new build is posted, repeat the same three steps and let the Finder replace the old copy when prompted. Your weekly data lives in Supabase, not in the app, so updates don't affect your standards uploads, audits, CTAs, or documents.

Troubleshooting

  • "App is damaged" or "cannot be opened" warnings: re-download the zip — partial downloads can corrupt the bundle. If it persists, contact Region 2 admin.
  • System Settings → Privacy & Security: if your Mac is on macOS 14 Sonoma or earlier and Gatekeeper still asks for permission, click "Open Anyway" once. Notarization should make this unnecessary on macOS 15 Sequoia and later.
  • Sign-in or live data issues: open the app, then Settings → Admin to confirm the backend mode and sign-in state.
