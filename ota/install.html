Over-the-Air Install

Tap the button below to install the latest version of Regional Leadership Hub on your iPhone or iPad. This is an internal Region 2 app — you must have a UDID-registered device and a valid ad-hoc provisioning profile to install.

📲 Install Regional Leadership Hub

Note: If the link doesn't work, copy this URL and open Safari on your iOS device: 

https://only21mil.github.io/regional-leadership-hub-site/ota/install.html

Troubleshooting

  • You must open this page in Safari — other browsers may not handle the itms-services:// protocol.
  • Your device UDID must be registered in the team's ad-hoc provisioning profile.
  • If prompted, go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management and trust the developer certificate.
  • After tapping install, go to your home screen — the app downloads and installs in the background.
