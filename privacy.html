Privacy Policy

Regional Leadership Hub (iOS)

Last updated: 2026-04-26 · Operator: DT of Tennessee (Discount Tire Region 2 leadership)

Who this applies to

Regional Leadership Hub is a private internal app used by authorized Discount Tire Region 2 (Tennessee) leadership for store visits, weekly standards review, CTAs, leadership documents, reporting, and follow-up planning. The app is not offered to the general public; it is distributed only to designated regional leaders via TestFlight or direct internal install.

This policy describes what the app collects, why, where it is stored, who can see it, and how to ask questions or request changes.

What the app collects

Account and identity

  • Email address — used for sign-in via Supabase Auth and to map to the region’s roster of authorized leaders.
  • Name — pulled from the regional roster so leadership documents and audits can be attributed correctly.
  • User ID — internal Supabase Auth user ID and app profile ID, used for session, role, and audit attribution.

Operational content you create or upload

  • Go See audit notes and item results
  • CTA (Call To Action) document details and follow-up notes
  • Leadership notes, weekly standards review entries, planning entries
  • Files you upload to private Supabase Storage buckets (PDFs, audit attachments, leadership documents)

What the app does not collect

  • Device location
  • Contacts, calendar, photos library, microphone, Bluetooth, health, or fitness data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA) — the app contains no advertising or tracking SDKs
  • Cross-app or cross-website tracking data
  • Purchases, financial information, search history, or browsing history
  • Crash, performance, or product-interaction analytics — the current app contains no third-party analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry SDK

How data is stored and protected

  • Sign-in tokens (Supabase publishable key, access token, refresh token) are stored in the iOS Keychain on the device.
  • Operational data is stored in a private Supabase project that is not publicly accessible. Access is restricted by row-level security: only active Region 2 leaders can read regional data, and only designated regional admins can create, update, or delete it.
  • Files uploaded by leaders are stored in private Supabase Storage buckets that mirror the same access rules.
  • Connections between the app and Supabase use HTTPS/TLS.

Who can see your data

Data entered into the app is visible to other authorized Region 2 leadership users in accordance with their assigned role (admin, read-write, read-only). Data is not shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is not sold.

The app uses Supabase as its backend infrastructure provider for authentication, database, and file storage. Supabase processes data on behalf of the regional leadership team strictly to provide that infrastructure.

How long data is retained

Operational data is retained for as long as it is useful to ongoing regional leadership work. Authorized regional admins can update or delete records. Authentication accounts can be deactivated by the regional admin when a leader leaves the role.

Children’s privacy

Regional Leadership Hub is intended only for adult leadership personnel. The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices and requests

If you are an authorized Region 2 leader and want to:

  • See what data is associated with your account
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Have your account deactivated and your personal identifiers removed when you leave the role

contact the regional administrator using the address on the Support page.

Changes to this policy

If the app changes what it collects or how data is handled (for example, if a future version adds camera import, push notifications, analytics, or crash reporting), this page will be updated and the “Last updated” date above will change.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the regional administrator listed on the Support page.
